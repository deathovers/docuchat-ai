# DocuChat AI User Guide

Welcome to DocuChat AI! This guide will help you get the most out of your multi-document assistant.

## 🌟 Getting Started

### 1. Uploading Documents
- Click the **Upload** button or drag and drop your PDF files into the interface.
- You can upload up to **10 documents** at a time.
- **Note:** Only text-based PDFs are supported. Scanned images or handwritten notes without an OCR layer will not be readable.

### 2. Asking Questions
- Once your documents are "Ready," type your question in the chat box.
- Example: *"What are the key risks mentioned in the annual report?"*
- Example: *"Compare the sustainability goals between Document A and Document B."*

### 3. Reading Responses
- The AI will respond in real-time.
- If the information is not in your documents, the AI will say: *"The answer was not found in the uploaded documents."* This prevents the AI from making things up.

### 4. Verifying with Citations
- At the end of each response, you will see a list of **Citations**.
- These tell you exactly which file and which page the AI used to generate the answer.

## ⚠️ Important Limitations

- **Session-Based Only:** Your documents and chat history are **temporary**. If you refresh the page or close your browser, the data is deleted for your security.
- **No OCR:** If you upload a PDF that is just a series of images (like a scan), the AI will not be able to "see" the text.
- **PDF Only:** Other file formats (Word, Excel, TXT) are not supported in the current version.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

- **"Invalid Session ID":** This happens if your session has expired. Please refresh and re-upload your documents.
- **Slow Responses:** Large documents (100+ pages) may take a few extra seconds to process initially.
- **No Answer Found:** Ensure your question is related to the content of the uploaded PDFs.
